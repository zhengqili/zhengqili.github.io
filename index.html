Zhengqi Li

I am a research scientist at Google Research. My research interests span 3D/4D computer vision, image-based rendering and computational photography, epsically for images and videos in the wild.

I received my CS Ph.D. degree at Cornell University where I was advised by Prof. Noah Snavely . I am a recipient of the CVPR 2019 Best Paper Hornorable Mention, 2020 Google Ph.D. Fellowship, 2020 Adobe Research Fellowship, 2021 Baidu Global Top 100 Chinese Rising Stars in AI, and 2023 CVPR Best Paper Award Candidate.
